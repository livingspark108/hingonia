Sri Krishna Balram Seva Trust

In 2016, the Government of Rajasthan and the Jaipur Nagar Nigam decided to partner with Hare Krishna Movement Jaipur, which runs the Sri Sri Krishna Balaram Mandir at the Akshaya Patra mid day meal centralized kitchen campus at Jagatpura, Jaipur. The full time missionaries of Hare Krishna Movement accepted the humble service to mother cows at Hingonia. A special purpose vehicle, Sri Krishna Balaram Seva Trust, was set up to run the Hingonia centre with the aid of Jaipur Nagar Nigam. This trust is a non profit organization, dedicated for serving the cattle at Hingonia. The Trust consists of 3 government officials, 3 independent trustees and 9 missionary trustees. The operations are managed by the full time missionaries of the Trust. Read More

Testimonials

Visibility To Our CSR Supporters

Donor's registered logo and name will be prominently displayed in form of hoardings & facades in the sponsored area ensuring brand visibility to the visitors. The primary sponsor will be given the maximum visibility on the exterior facade and rest of the supporters will be provided with a specified space termed as the donor wall.

RSDCL

The number of cows here has grown to over 18,000, making it challenging to feed them on time. Rajasthan Solarpark Development Company Limited (RSDCL) has helped by providing a TMR (Total Mixed Ration) Fodder Feeding Machine that mixes green fodder, dry fodder, and concentrate, ensuring all cows get equal nutrition. With this machine, we can now feed 3,000 cows every day.

RIICO

RIICO's CSR support has helped the Hingonia Cattle Rehabilitation Center in preserving soil and groundwater while promoting environmental sustainability. Their contribution of a dead animal incinerator machine ensures efficient waste management, reflecting RIICO's commitment to creating a healthier environment for all.

GCL

With the generous CSR support from Gayson & Company Limited, our Hingonia Cattle Rehabilitation Center was able to provide arrangements for 4,500 cows in a day, including dry fodder, green fodder, and concentrate. This support has been instrumental in our mission to serve the 18,000 cows in our care. We extend our heartfelt gratitude for their contribution towards our cause.

H.G.

H.G. Foundation's generous support in building a 44,000 sqft shelter home for 500 stray and abandoned cows is truly commendable. The brick flooring ensures their comfort and safety, particularly during rainy days. Solar lights have been installed to provide care for the cows at night, especially the elderly and sick ones. Additionally, shade-providing trees have been planted to offer relief from the heat during hot weather, further enhancing their well-being.

IOCL

Through the generous CSR contribution of the Indian Oil Corporation Limited, a state-of-the-art biogas plant with a capacity of 100 MTPD has been established within the Hingonia premises. This sustainable initiative underscores our commitment to environmental conservation and renewable energy utilization. The biogas is used to create a Porridge for the ill cows, and Akshaya Patra utilizes it for serving mid-day meals to 1.5 lakh students.

AFL

Aavas Financiers Limited's support for the plantation of 2000 trees promotes sustainable agriculture and provides nutritious fruits for the cattle. The installation of a 200 KW solar grid power plant reduces electricity costs, reliance on non-renewable energy, and the carbon footprint. Brick flooring in the Nandi Shala improves hygiene, reduces infections, and enhances cattle well-being, showcasing Aavas's commitment to CSR.
