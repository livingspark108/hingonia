Our Mission and Efforts

Every day, a fleet of Jaipur Nagar Nigam ambulances transports nearly 100 cattle from Jaipur city to our rehabilitation centre. Among them, over 30 arrive in critical condition, requiring immediate medical intervention and specialized care. Many families, due to poverty or lack of resources, are unable to care for their cattles, leading to abandonment. Hingonia stands as a vital solution, offering compassionate care to these animals while addressing the larger environmental and social challenges.

Creating Positive Change

By partnering with Sri Krishna Balram Seva Trust and the Government of Rajasthan, we are dedicated to transforming Hingonia Cow Rehabilitation Centre into a model for sustainability and animal welfare. Together, we aim to:

  • Improve the health and well-being of stray and injured cows.
  • Promote sustainable practices that protect the environment.
  • Engage communities in animal welfare and environmental awareness.

Make a Difference

Your CSR contributions will directly support:

  • Infrastructure improvements for shelter and medical care.
  • Nutritional programs to ensure proper fodder and health.
  • Development of sustainable practices such as tree plantations, solar energy grids, and eco-friendly initiatives.
